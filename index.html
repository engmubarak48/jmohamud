Bio

I am a software engineer and have worked in both academia and industry. Currently, I am working with Moustapha Cisse on representation learning and developing interesting unsupervised representation learning methods. Also, I hold double masters: one in Artificial Intelligence from the prestigious Africa Masters in Machine Intelligence program sponsored by Google and Facebook, and another in Electrical & Electronic Engineering from Anadolu University. I am goal-oriented, hard-working, result-driven and possess excellent communication, interpersonal skills. I am very adaptable to any environment as I have worked with different people from various countries.

Fun fact: I am from Somaliland (an unrecognized state in the East of Africa, you should come visit when you have sometime and get to see the Laas Geel where most of the early creatures lived : )
